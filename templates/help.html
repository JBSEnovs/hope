Help & User Guide

This help guide provides information on how to use the Medical AI Assistant application effectively.

Getting Started with Medical AI Assistant

Welcome to the Medical AI Assistant, your personal health management and medical information tool.

Overview

The Medical AI Assistant provides several key features:

  • Dashboard - View your health metrics, medication adherence, and upcoming activities
  • Medication Management - Track your medications and record when they're taken
  • AI Chatbot - Ask medical questions and get AI-powered responses
  • Symptom Analysis - Describe symptoms to get potential diagnoses
  • Treatment Information - Learn about treatment options for medical conditions
Navigation

Use the navigation bar at the top of the page to access different features:

  • Home - Return to the welcome page
  • Dashboard - View your health overview
  • Chatbot - Interact with the AI assistant
  • Medications - Manage your medications
  • Tools - Access additional features like document upload and settings
Medical Disclaimer: This application provides information for educational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare providers for medical concerns.

Using the Dashboard

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your health metrics, medication adherence, and upcoming activities.

Key Components
  • Health Overview - Shows the current date and your basic health status
  • Stats Cards - Quick view of your medication adherence, blood pressure, active medications, and upcoming activities
  • Blood Pressure History - Chart showing your blood pressure trends over time
  • Medication Adherence - Visual representation of your medication adherence rate
  • Upcoming Activities - List of scheduled health-related activities
  • Current Medications - Overview of your active medications with quick actions
Actions You Can Take
  • Mark medications as taken directly from the dashboard
  • View medication details by clicking the info button
  • See upcoming appointments and activities
  • Track your medication adherence progress
  • Monitor changes in your health metrics over time
Tip: Use the dashboard daily to keep track of your medications and monitor your health status effectively.

Medication Management

The Medication Management page helps you track your medications, set schedules, and monitor your adherence.

Main Features
  • Current Medications Table - Lists all your active medications
  • Medication Details - View comprehensive information about each medication
  • Adherence Statistics - Visual representation of your medication adherence
  • Medication Schedule - Calendar view of upcoming medication doses
How to Add a Medication
  1. Click the "Add Medication" button at the top of the page
  2. Fill in the medication details (name, dosage, frequency, start date)
  3. Add optional information like end date and notes
  4. Set reminders if needed
  5. Click "Save Medication" to add it to your list
Managing Medications
  • Mark as Taken - Click the checkmark button to record that you've taken a medication
  • View Details - Click the eye button to see comprehensive information about a medication
  • Delete - Remove medications you no longer take
Tip: Set up reminders for your medications to improve your adherence rate.

AI Chatbot

The AI Chatbot allows you to ask medical questions and get AI-powered responses.

How to Use the Chatbot
  1. Type your medical question in the input field
  2. Click the "Send" button or press Enter
  3. The AI will process your question and provide a response
  4. You can ask follow-up questions to get more specific information
AI Provider Selection

You can choose from different AI providers:

  • BlackboxAI (Default) - General-purpose medical AI
  • OpenAI - Advanced language model for detailed responses
  • Google Gemini - Google's medical knowledge model
  • Cohere - Specialized medical information model
Additional Features
  • Voice Input - Click the microphone button to speak your question
  • Visualizations - Check the "Include Visualization" option to get charts when applicable
  • Voice Response - Enable text-to-speech to have the AI read responses aloud
Note: The AI provides information for educational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Advanced Features

The Medical AI Assistant includes several advanced features to help you manage your health.

Document Repository

Upload medical documents to enhance the AI's knowledge:

  • Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT
  • Maximum file size: 16MB
  • Documents are processed to extract medical information
Image Analysis

Upload medical images for AI-assisted analysis:

  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, TIFF, BMP
  • Apply various enhancements to improve image quality
  • Receive AI interpretation of image metrics
Voice Interface

Interact with the AI using voice commands:

  • Record voice input to describe symptoms or ask questions
  • Process transcriptions for different types of analysis
  • Listen to AI responses through speech synthesis
Settings

Customize the application to your preferences:

  • Theme - Choose between light, dark, or system theme
  • Language - Set your preferred language for the interface
  • Notifications - Configure how you receive medication reminders
  • Privacy - Manage your data and sharing preferences
